Problem Statement

━━━━━━━━━
The previous inventory management system was a really rudimentary MVP that did not cover all the basic needs to run the business. Therefore, the operation assistants could not properly manage inventory, track ordering items, and analyze data within the system.

 

 

The Goals

━━━━━━━━━

Project Goals

Design an inventory management system by creating a system of UI components that all engineering teams could leverage and decrease development costs.

User Goals

Simplifiy workflows and save 30% time when operation assistants manage inventory.

Business Goals

Understand buyers’ demand and trends to forecast demand, analyze buying patterns and plan for the future.

    How We Discover Problems

    ━━━━━━━━━

    I had to go to the beggining of of everything, so I set up a list of different stakeholders included operation assistants and data scientists who have already using the previous inventory management system. I searched for people who were willing to:

  • Share their thoughts openly
  • Tell us about their problems and needs with 0% fear
  • Complain! The more energy the better!

    •  

    The Problems in E-commerce Integrations

    ━━━━━━━━━

     

     

    After I did user research and understood the inventory management flow in our company, I listed problems and needs in each step:

     

    Inventory Management

    Problems

  • The operation assistants have to update stocks in spreadsheets, then manually import to the system. They felt overwhelmed by daily orders.

    • Needs

  • The operation assistants want to manage and centralize all of the products and stock lists within a system.

    •  

     

    Order Processing and Tracking

    Problems

  • The operation assistants have to order and track items from multiple sales channels, which is time-consuming and inefficient.

    • Needs

  • The operation assistants want to order and track all the stocks in one channel.

    •  

     

    Data and Reporting

    Problems

  • Due to the scattered data in different channels, the operation assistants and data scientists can’t easily forecast demand.

    • Needs

  • An intergrated and real-time dashboard to understand customers’ behavior and forecast demand.
