I'm Constance and I'm in my first year of grad school at Indiana University's HCI/d program.

I graduated with a bachelor's degree in psychology from National Taiwan University, which equipped me with understanding of user behavior and being able to take that information to design delightful experiences. With so many emerging technologies, there are even more opportunities to make life just a tiny bit easier for the people using it. My design goal is to reduce complexity everywhere I go and make life easier.

Outside of the office, I'm a big coffee fan and film buff. In my spare time you'll find me scouting new coffee shops in the city or continuing my way down the top hits on the IMDB 250.
